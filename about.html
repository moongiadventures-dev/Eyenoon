About

The optical
editorial

Eye Noon Optical is a trendsetting optical boutique offering a refined eyewear experience both online and at our Los Angeles storefront.

Built by innovators and forward-thinkers, our mission is to continuously elevate the eyewear shopping experience through quality, craftsmanship, and exceptional, personalized service.

Our brands

We pride ourselves on offering a carefully curated selection of high-end designer eyewear and luxury sunglasses, featuring exclusive imports from brands like CUTLER AND GROSS, LINDA FARROW, L.G.R, RETROSUPERFUTURE, YUICHI TOYAMA., and AKILA. To guarantee optimal vision and comfort, our premium frames are expertly fitted and paired with ZEISS precision lenses.

Our services & expertise

Whether you are shopping from the comfort of your home or visiting us in-person, we are committed to providing exceptional customer care.

In addition to offering comprehensive eye exams by appointment, our team possesses deep technical expertise in eyewear maintenance.

As demonstrated to our community on our dedicated YouTube channel, we specialize in precision eyewear care, including custom-made clip-ons, broken hinge replacements, and professional lens handling.
